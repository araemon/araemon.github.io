Version 0.1.1 (â±2019.5.25)

White noise was introduced. It is very difficult to make a real white noise. In real time, it is determined that the load is too large to process. The pseudo white noise using random numbers is adopted. If you think about it now, there may be a way to play the prepared sound source.

Well, this video is trying to reproduce the sound of the sea. At the time I thought it was very similar, but now I hear it sounds much louder than it sounds. I thought it was necessary to have a low pass filter to cut the high frequency that was smooth.

Low pass filters are easy to implement in electronic circuits, but they are not as easy to program. You have to learn difficult learning such as FIR filter and IIR filter. Fourier Transform, Euler's Formula, Discrete Fourier, z Transform, Linear Interpolation, etc. will appear, and if you have any doubts you will not move forward. It is a world where you can not do without thinking that you have been trapped.

It goes without saying that it took more than a month to start the low pass filter after this.
